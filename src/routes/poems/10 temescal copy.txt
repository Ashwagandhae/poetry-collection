##
Magnet rattling racket,
trucks retracting back. 
Wailing table legs pulled over
muttered laser guns as 
heart rate moniters and 
zip ties unchain their saws:
the birds emerge.  

Neon pole-ball sunrises
the road ocean flows. 


##
the magnet rattle,
truck backing,
laser guns
screeching table leg
hear rate moniter,
zip ties,
chainsaws
tiny motorcycles.

above the fishing pole sun lights up the water
the road ocean flowing beside
